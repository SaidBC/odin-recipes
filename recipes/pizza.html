HERE IS THE PIZZA

Pizza is an Italian food that was created in Italy (The Naples area). It is made with different toppings. Some of the most common toppings are cheese, sausages, pepperoni, vegetables, tomatoes, spices and herbs and basil. These toppings are added over a piece of bread covered with sauce.

Ingredianst

  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded or fresh)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Sliced bell peppers (green, red, yellow)
  • Sliced onions (red or yellow)
  • Mushrooms (sliced)
  • Cooked sausage (Italian sausage, chorizo, etc.)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Pineapple chunks (for Hawaiian pizza)
  • Black olives (sliced)

steps

  1. Start with a crust
  2. Add a sauce
  3. Add some veggies
  4. Try some fruit on your pizza
  5. Add some protein
  6. Add cheese
  7. Bake your creation in a hot oven (450 F or above)
